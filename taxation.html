Taxation Services

Our taxation services ensure full compliance with UAE tax laws while optimizing your company’s tax position.

From VAT registration and corporate tax filing to strategic tax advisory, Darbia supports businesses with end-to-end tax compliance solutions.

Our Taxation Services Include

UAE VAT Registration & Filing

Assistance with VAT registration and regular returns submission as per FTA guidelines.

We ensure timely VAT return filings, handle input and output tax reconciliations, and provide full compliance documentation in accordance with UAE law.

Corporate Tax (CT) Filing & Compliance

Preparation and filing of corporate tax returns under the UAE's Corporate Tax regime.

We help assess tax obligations, prepare necessary documentation, and ensure accurate filings with the Federal Tax Authority (FTA).

Withholding Tax Support

Guidance on withholding tax obligations on cross-border payments.

We ensure your business remains compliant when dealing with international payments, and we help you understand exemptions, rates, and documentation requirements.

Tax Audit Representation

Acting on your behalf during tax audits and addressing queries raised by authorities.

Our experts liaise directly with auditors, ensure that necessary documents are provided, and help you respond effectively to all tax authority inquiries.

Tax Planning & Advisory

Strategic tax planning to reduce liabilities and improve financial outcomes.

We develop tax-efficient structures, evaluate deductions and credits, and guide long-term planning to optimize tax benefits across your business operations.

Tax Residency Certificate Assistance

Help in obtaining TRCs for individuals and companies for double tax treaty benefits.

We handle the entire TRC application process including documentation, submission to relevant authorities, and follow-up to ensure approval.

Economic Substance Regulation (ESR) Support

Ensuring your business meets ESR requirements through reporting and advisory services.

We assess whether your entity falls under ESR scope, prepare reports, submit notifications, and provide recommendations to fulfill substance tests.
