Accounting & Bookkeeping

Streamline your finances with accuracy and integrity.

At Darbia, we go beyond just numbers — we provide clarity, ensure compliance with UAE regulations, and support your business growth with strategic financial insights.

From day-to-day bookkeeping to full financial reporting, our expert team helps you stay organized, audit-ready, and confident in your decision-making.

Our Accounting & Bookkeeping Services Include

General Ledger Maintenance

Recording all financial transactions in the company’s ledger to ensure accurate reporting. Our team ensures every credit and debit is correctly categorized, allowing for real-time financial tracking and audit readiness. We also implement robust checks and balances to help identify inconsistencies early and maintain transparency across financial records.

Accounts Payable & Receivable Management

Managing incoming and outgoing payments to maintain healthy cash flow. We streamline invoice tracking, payment scheduling, and follow-ups to ensure your business stays liquid and vendors stay satisfied. Our automation tools reduce human error and speed up transaction processing, helping your business maintain financial agility.

Bank Reconciliation

Matching the company’s financial records with bank statements to ensure accuracy. This process helps uncover errors, prevent fraud, and maintain a solid financial foundation. Regular reconciliation reduces discrepancies and supports a clearer view of your real-time cash position.

Financial Statement Preparation

Preparing essential reports like balance sheets, income statements, and cash flow statements. These reports help stakeholders understand business performance and support regulatory filings. Our statements adhere to international accounting standards and are ready for audits or board presentations.

Payroll Processing

Accurate calculation and disbursement of employee salaries, benefits, and deductions. We ensure compliance with local labor laws and timely submissions to avoid penalties and maintain staff satisfaction. Our payroll services also include payslip generation, statutory reporting, and leave accrual management.

Inventory & Asset Management

Monitoring and recording inventory and fixed assets to control costs and ensure proper valuation. We help you track depreciation, asset lifecycle, and inventory turnover for better decision-making. With real-time tracking and inventory alerts, your business can prevent stock-outs or overstocking.

Monthly / Quarterly / Year-End Closing

Finalizing accounts at regular intervals to prepare for financial analysis or audits. Our systematic approach ensures all records are reviewed, reconciled, and summarized accurately. We help identify anomalies early and ensure smooth financial closure without last-minute surprises.

VAT-Compliant Bookkeeping

Recording transactions in line with UAE VAT laws for proper filing and audit readiness. We handle input/output tax calculations, maintain VAT records, and ensure timely submissions. Our processes ensure that you're always ready for tax audits with accurate and complete documentation.

Financial Data Migration

Securely transferring financial data from legacy systems to modern accounting platforms. Our structured migration process minimizes errors, protects data integrity, and ensures continuity. We perform pre- and post-migration audits to confirm that nothing is lost or duplicated in the process.
